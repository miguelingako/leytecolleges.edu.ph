Anyag Sinirangan Cultural and Performing Arts—Terpsichore Oath-Taking Ceremony




Anyag Sinirangan Cultural and Performing Arts—Terpsichore Oath-Taking Ceremony "𝖨𝗍'𝗌 𝗇𝗈𝗍 𝗍𝗁𝖾 𝗈𝖺𝗍𝗁 𝗍𝗁𝖺𝗍 𝗆𝖺𝗄𝖾𝗌 𝗎𝗌 𝖻𝖾𝗅𝗂𝖾𝗏𝖾 𝗍𝗁𝖾 𝗆𝖺𝗇, 𝖻𝗎𝗍 𝗍𝗁𝖾 𝗆𝖺𝗇 𝗍𝗁𝖾 𝗈𝖺𝗍𝗁"

- 𝗔𝗲𝘀𝗰𝗵𝘆𝗹𝘂𝘀

The Oath-Taking Ceremony of the newly elected officers of Anyag Sinirangan Cultural and Performing Arts—Terpsichore took place at Leyte Colleges San Jose Campus on October 5, 2024, solemnly swearing their commitment to the new era of leadership.

With pride and dedication, each member pledged to honor and commit to promoting rich cultural heritage through their artistic expressions. This moment marked a new chapter in their journey, celebrating the vibrance of Anyag Sinirangan—Terpsichore and the promise of unity and creativity.

Similarly, following their signing of the Pledge of Commitment, the Anyag Sinirangan Cultural and Performing Arts—Terpsichore exhibited an amusing intermission number.

Teachers’ Day




For this year's Teachers’ Day, the Leyte Colleges Supreme Student Council (SSC) is proud and excited to have this wonderful opportunity to express their great love and care for their hardworking, passionate and dedicated instructors.

In partnership with the Tacloban City Community Livelihood Extension Program (CLEP), and Student Affairs and Services Office (SASO), the SSC will be facilitating an event the," Instructors' Pamper Session" where manicure, massage and haircut services ARE FREE. This will be held on the 11th of October 2024 at the Leyte Colleges Paterno Campus, Room 6 & 7.

This event is a gift from all LCians to you. We thank you from the bottom of our hearts for your guidance and support.

We hope to see you there! >

Leyte Colleges conducts First Friday Mass




Leyte Colleges held the First Friday Mass Celebration organized by the College of Education today, October 4, 2024. it was presided over by Parish Priest Fr. Hercolano Tabada at the LC Paterno campus.

The said event was attended by faculty, staff and students along with the Anyag Sinirangan Cultural and Performing Arts Chorale.

Faculty, staff, and students gathered the school community for a moment of devotion and divinity.

On the other hand, the COE announced that there will be an upcoming United Nations Month Cebration with series of activities on October 24, 2024 at Leyte Colleges Paterno Campus.
